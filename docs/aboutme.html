ABOUT PENTOOLGAMER

I am Emerson Hernandez a 24-year-old freelance graphic designer, digital artist, vector artist, and video editor from the Philippines, dedicated to creating unique and engaging works of art.

As a freelance digital artist, I focus on creating visually compelling and innovative designs, blending my love for creativity and technology. I also have a strong foundation in graphic design and digital art, as well as a knowledge about understanding of computer programming, basic web design, and computer assembly.

I am a third-year college student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at City of Malabon University. This diverse skill set allows me to approach my art from a technical perspective, continually improving and evolving. My work is inspired by creating royal portraits through vector art, graphic design, and digital painting, transforming classical styles into modern, captivating pieces. My goal is to create powerful, vibrant, and visually striking works that connect with others.

Commission Process & Shipping Information

I process commission orders Monday through Friday, with weekends off. Typically, commissions are completed within 3 to 5 business days. However, if there is a delay due to high demand or other circumstances, I will keep you updated.

For domestic commissions, delivery typically takes 1 - 5 days after completion. For international commissions, shipping may take 10 - 30 business days. Please be patient, as shipping times may vary. If you need expedited shipping, feel free to reach out for options and costs. Please note that for international orders, shipping costs do not cover any potential import duties or tariffs.
